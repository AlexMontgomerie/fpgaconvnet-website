fpgaConvNet is a toolflow designed by the Intelligent Digital Systems Lab from Imperial College London, which maps CNN models onto FPGA devices. The toolflow automates the whole design process, and optimises the accelerator architecture in order to get the maximum performance for a model and FPGA pair.
